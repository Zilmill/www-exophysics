About Exophysics

License

The entire project, including code and website, is in the public domain to make it available to as many as possible. All code can be found on GitHub.

Finances

There's no paywall, advertisements, analytics, or anything that might distract from the project. My motivation and my payment is to see what kind of beautiful creations you come up with.

If you wish to contribute human currency to the creator of Exophysics though, please donate on my patreon page: https://www.patreon.com/superhut. I'm an independent open source software programmer and digital artist, but also trapped in a human body. As such, I do require a minimal amount of human currency to sustain myself and keep my attention on creative endeavours. Help me pay the bills so I can keep building more projects like these. :) Any contributions are deeply appreciated.
