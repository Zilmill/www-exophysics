Exophysics Exhibition

This is the museum of explored exophysics universes. Please send in any contributions via the human-era "email" technology to curator໑exophysics.net.

You can use mouse clicks and the "." and "space" keys to interact with particles, though every universe is a little different, and it might not work everywhere.
